Little choices, big changes

In celebration of earth week, we’re sharing simple planet-positive tips for your next Starbucks visit. Together we can help reduce waste and make an impact.

Earn Rewards with a personal cup

Bring a clean personal cup into a café and you’ll get 10¢ off your drink order, plus 25 Stars for Starbucks® Rewards members. Stars add up to free food and drinks.*

A trailblazing coffee returns
